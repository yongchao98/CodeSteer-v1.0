The initial total score is 0. Given a string consisting of letters A and B, scan from front to back:

1. If there are two or more consecutive A's, add 1 to the total score.
2. If there are two or more consecutive B's, add 2 to the total score.

What is the total score of the string ABBBA?
Return your answer with the format <<<number>>>, like <<<5>>> means the total score is 5.
